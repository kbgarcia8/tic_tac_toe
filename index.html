TIC TAC TOE

The game is played by 2 players each represented by a marker, X and O respectively.
Players take turn putting a marker on wherever the player wants. The first player to
get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins the game!

Scoreboard:

Player 1 [X]: 0

Player 2 [O]: 0

Please enjoy the game!
